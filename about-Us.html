About The Director

In today's competitive business world, get India's finest brand evangelists who play a critical role in driving business success.

Ravineet Singh Marwah (RSM), a professional investor and a successful renowned brand evangelist with over 15 years of experience in the sales & growth, has worked in various industries such as Autotech & Fintech, e-commerce, Web3.0, Fashion & Jewellery, Luxury Lifestyle, Luxury & Premium Automobiles. He is known as the best motivational speaker in India, an international business consultant, an expert in agile change management, motivation in business management, a business mentor, a personal brand consultant, and a business strategy expert. He is also known as the best business coach in India and a Web 3.0 metaverse expert.Throughout his career, he has had the privilege of joining global organizations at their early stages and has been a part of the leadership teams of exceedingly successful brands that have grown into billion-dollar companies.

Throughout his career, he has had the privilege of joining global organizations at their early stages and has been a part of the leadership teams of exceedingly successful brands that have grown into billion-dollar companies. As a luxury evangelist, RSM had the honor of associating with some of the world's best labels, he played an instrumental role in CarDekho to become unicorn & Launched luxury auto e-commerce, lead highest national sales in Audi & Porsche India, secured exclusive rights with Armani in GCC & MENA region, launched exclusive Fendi Casa and Ralph Lauren Home India and the Middle East. Academically, he is an alumnus of Indian Institute of Management Ahmedabad, Narsee Monjee Institute of Management studies Mumbai & Rajasthan University. Throughout his professional experience he has achieved global certifications like Web3.0 Blockchain Council, Cisco, Microsoft. Currently, he is building a Web 3.0-based luxury premium fashion and jewelry marketplace called Indyverse.

The platform curates Indian Ethnic Fashion and gives customers access to the unique stories and fresh designs of undiscovered talent and established designers that make clothes and jewelry. Indyverse is one of the first marketplaces to provide a customized tailoring & styling solution with measurements taken at home. It takes you into the Metaverse with digital avatars, virtual trial rooms, NFTs, and membership to an online Web3.0 community. In addition to his entrepreneurial pursuits, he also has extensive mentorship experience across corporates and management schools. Whether you are a startup looking to establish your brand, or an established business looking to scale up, RSM is here to help.
